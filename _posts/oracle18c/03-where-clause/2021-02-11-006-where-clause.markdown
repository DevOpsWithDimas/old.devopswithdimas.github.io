## Predicates / Conditional

Predicate / Conditional ada beberapa operator yang bisa digunakan diantaranya

1. Comparison Predicate
2. Floating Point Condition
3. Like Predicate
4. Between Predicate
5. Nulless Predicate
6. Exist Condition
7. In Condition
8. Logical Statement
